THE CHALLENGE

This Is Not a Fitness Program

A 75-day mental toughness test with real financial stakes. Five daily non-negotiables, 75 straight days. Complete them all — you win. Miss even one — you're out and you lose money. Simple as that.

NON-NEGOTIABLE
NO ALCOHOL
NO DRUGS
NO CHEAT MEALS
EVERY SINGLE DAY

The 5 Rules

Complete all five, every day, for 75 days straight. These are the proposed defaults — you can propose modifications when you sign up.

RULE 01
Follow Your Diet — Zero Exceptions
Declare your diet before Day 1. No changes mid-challenge. No cheat meals. No alcohol. No drugs. You define it, you stick to it.
RULE 02
Two 45-Minute Workouts
Two separate sessions, 45 min each. One must be outdoors — parks, trails, sidewalks, courts. Intentional exercise only. A purposeful walk counts. A stroll does not.
RULE 03
Drink One Gallon of Water
128 oz (one gallon) every day. Coffee and tea don't count. Just water.
RULE 04
Read 10 Pages of Non-Fiction
Physical book or e-reader — 10 pages minimum. Audiobooks don't count. Log what you read daily.
RULE 05
Take a Daily Progress Photo
One timestamped photo every day. For your own accountability — doesn't need to be shared.
⚠

Miss any rule on any day = eliminated. No grace days. No make-ups. No exceptions. You can propose rule modifications when you sign up.
