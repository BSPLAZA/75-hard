Overview

A Telegram bot that posts one daily card each morning. All participants interact with it throughout the day. The card updates in-place as people complete tasks, keeping the group chat clean for conversation.

Zero-friction tracking

Completing a check-in should be faster than not doing it. One tap for water, two taps for workouts.

Social accountability

Everyone sees the card filling in throughout the day. Nobody gets called out. The progress speaks.

Clean group chat

One updating card per day, not 30+ bot messages. Reading cards are the only extra posts, and they earn their space.

Adaptive personality

The bot feels like a 6th group member, not a corporate wellness tool. Phase C adds Claude-powered contextual messages.

Data for the long haul

Structured tracking enables Phase C features: photo timelines, reading journals, web dashboards.

Built-in feedback loop

Users submit bugs and suggestions via commands. Bryan reviews in Telegram, builds in Claude Code, deploys on Fly.io.

🔥

The 5 Rules

Every day for 75 consecutive days. Miss one task on any day = elimination.

🏋 Two 45-min Workouts

One must be outdoor, one must be indoor. Type and location logged via bot.

💧 Drink a Gallon of Water

16 cups (128 oz). Tracked via tap-to-increment on the daily card.

🍽 Follow Your Diet

Your choice of diet. No alcohol. No cheat meals. One-tap confirm (toggleable for honesty).

📖 Read 10 Pages

Non-fiction only. Bot remembers your current book. Share a takeaway each day.

📸 Progress Photo

Daily selfie sent via DM. Private by default. Stored for end-of-challenge transformation timeline.

Onboarding Flow

Step 1: Group Setup

Bryan creates group, adds 5 people + bot. Bot gets can_pin_messages admin right.

Step 2: Welcome Message

Bot posts: "DM me to register." Shows live counter: "Waiting for: Gaurav, Dev (3/5)"

Step 3: DM Registration

User taps bot link, types name. Bot fuzzy-matches against participant list. Stores Telegram ID mapping.

Step 4: All In

At 5/5: "Everyone's in! First card drops at 7 AM ET tomorrow." Pins the Rules & FAQ.

❓

Pinned FAQ

Pinned permanently in the group. Covers rules, bot usage, and common questions.

What if I work out after midnight?

Log it for the day you're in. If it's 1 AM Tuesday, it counts for Tuesday.

What if I forget to log something?

Backfill until noon the next day. Bot reminds at 11 PM if you have unchecked tasks.

What counts as a "cup" of water?

8 oz / ~250 ml. A gallon = 16 cups = 128 oz.

Can I change my diet mid-challenge?

Yes. Follow A diet. Whatever you commit to, follow it. No alcohol and no cheat meals are non-negotiable.

What if I fail?

DM /fail. Get $1 back per day completed. Rest stays in the prize pool. You stay in the group.

I tapped water too many times

/water set [number] to correct your cup count.

I logged the wrong workout

/workout_undo to clear your last workout, then re-log via the card.

Where is today's card?

Type /card and the bot will link you. It's also pinned at the top of the chat.

I have an idea / Something is broken

/suggest [idea] or /bug [description]. Bryan will see it.

Edge Cases

Old Card Taps

Yesterday's card still in chat — if before noon grace period, apply to previous day. After noon: "This card has expired."

Failure Flow

User DMs /fail → "Type CONFIRM" → posted to group with respect → $X returned → row removed from card → user stays in group.

Race Conditions

Two simultaneous water taps: both write to SQLite (source of truth), retry message edit once on conflict.

Photo Edge Cases

Multiple photos: latest wins. Non-photo files: rejected. Retake: just send another.

Book Changes

Bot remembers current book. "Still reading X?" → [Yes] / [New book]. Tracks finished books for stats.

Diet Toggle

Tapping diet again un-confirms it. Honesty-first design for cheat meal situations.

Group Migration

Telegram auto-migrates to supergroup: bot handles MigrateToChatId update, stores new chat_id, keeps running.

Bot Downtime

On restart: checks day, posts card if missing, SQLite preserved on Fly.io volume. FAQ: "Honor system until it's back."

Unregistered DM

Tapping Read/Photo without DM registration: "I can't DM you yet! Tap the link to start."

Day 75 — End

Celebration card instead of normal card. Final stats, books read, prize pool distribution. Bot goes dormant.
